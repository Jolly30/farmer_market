About Farmer Market

Farmer Market is a web-based platform that connects local farmers and buyers directly. Our system allows users to browse agricultural products, send purchase requests, communicate securely through chat, and complete deals efficiently.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify agricultural trading by removing unnecessary middlemen, increasing transparency, and helping local farmers reach more customers. We aim to create a reliable and user-friendly digital marketplace for fresh farm products.

Platform Features

Browse Products

Buyers can explore product categories and view detailed product information including price and available quantity.

Send Requests

Buyers can send product requests with selected quantity, payment method, and delivery options.

Secure Chat

After a request is accepted, buyers and sellers can communicate through a built-in chat system.

Seller Verification

Farmers must apply and get approved by the administrator before selling products.

Account Management

Users can manage profile information, update security settings, and switch between buyer and seller modes.

Admin Dashboard

The administrator monitors applications, categories, product approvals, and platform statistics.

Technology Stack

  • Backend: Python (Flask Framework)
  • Database: SQLite
  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 5, JavaScript
  • Template Engine: Jinja2
  • Authentication: Email OTP verification & password hashing
